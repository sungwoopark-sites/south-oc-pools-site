About South OC Pools

Family-Owned & Operated Since 2005

Since 2005, South OC Pools Service and Repairs has provided high-quality swimming pool and spa repair and maintenance services throughout Orange County, California. As a small business, our team of experts is committed to delivering excellent customer service.

South OC Pools is an owner-operated company, and we treat every job with the care and attention it deserves to ensure 100% satisfaction. Our approach is straightforward: show up on time, do quality work, and keep communication clear and honest.

Every technician on our team undergoes a comprehensive background check and professional training before ever stepping foot on a job site. We take your trust seriously — and treat your home like it's our own.
